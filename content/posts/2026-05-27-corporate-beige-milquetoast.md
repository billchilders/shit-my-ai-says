## Field Notes

Some of them still feel like they were designed by a risk committee in a windowless conference room, then moisturized with policy language until every trace of erotic charge was fully neutralized.

Claude is useful. Claude is capable. Claude is also, apparently, Corporate Beige Milquetoast.
